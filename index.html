Narayanan Muthiah

Narayanan Muthiah is a highly accomplished professional with over 35+ years of experience in software engineering and technology. With a strong background in technical roles including software engineer, lead engineer, and technical architect, Narayanan has honed his skills working on various projects for clients in the banking and financial services industries.
Starting his career with Tata Consultancy Services in 2002, Narayanan gained expertise in software development and project management. His decade-long tenure at TCS saw him rise through the ranks to become a Technical Architect, leading teams on complex projects. In 2013, Narayanan joined Verizon as a Lead Engineer, where he gained experience in cloud computing, DevOps, and big data technologies.
Currently a Senior Manager at Amazon Web Services (AWS), Narayanan is responsible for leading a team of Solutions Architects who help customers implement and adopt cloud technologies. His technical proficiency in Java, Python, C++, agile methodologies, software design patterns, and cloud architecture makes him a valuable asset to any organization in the technology industry.
In his role as a Board of Advisor for Webisdom, Narayanan's extensive experience in the technology industry and expertise in agile methodologies, software design patterns, and cloud architecture have aided Webisdom in developing innovative solutions for its clients and provided valuable insights to enhance technology strategies and stay ahead of the competition.
